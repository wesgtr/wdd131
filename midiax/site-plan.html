Overview

Purpose

To present and promote the digital services offered by MídiaX Studio, such as photo editing, video production, and paid traffic campaigns. This site will also serve as a portfolio and contact platform for clients.

Audience

Small businesses, content creators, influencers, and professionals looking for high-quality media services to grow their online presence.

Dynamic elements

A JavaScript image gallery with filters by category, a testimonial carousel with transition effects, a quote calculator for paid traffic campaigns, and a contact form with validation.
